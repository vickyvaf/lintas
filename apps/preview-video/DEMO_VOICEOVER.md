# Lintas Video Demo — Voiceover Script (ElevenLabs TTS Optimized)

This document contains the voiceover script for the Lintas hackathon video demo. Each section is designed to be copy-pasted directly into ElevenLabs TTS.

---

### Scene 1: Introduction (Duration: ~10s)
> **Text for TTS:**
> "Welcome to Lintas, a seamless mobile bridge that connects on-chain Stellar assets directly to real-world retail payments in Indonesia. Today, we will demonstrate how a user can spend stable assets to pay any retail merchant instantly."

---

### Scene 2: Home Tab & Freighter Wallet Connection (Duration: ~15s)
> **Text for TTS:**
> "First, we connect our Freighter wallet. Once connected, Lintas displays real-time balances of USDC and native XLM. The wallet automatically synchronizes the active network environment—whether Testnet or Mainnet. All calculations dynamically adapt to the user's preferred display currency, in either IDR or USD."

---

### Scene 3: Camera Scanning & Quote Engine (Duration: ~15s)
> **Text for TTS:**
> "To pay a merchant, we go to the Scan tab. The app launches the camera instantly. We scan a standard Indonesian QRIS code. Lintas parses the merchant metadata, retrieves live exchange rates, and calculates the exact amount of crypto required to settle the transaction."

---

### Scene 4: Gallery Upload & Personal Receive QR (Duration: ~15s)
> **Text for TTS:**
> "If the camera is not available, users can easily upload a QRIS invoice image from their gallery. Additionally, Lintas supports receiving payments by generating a personal QR code linked to the Freighter address with optional custom amount requests."

---

### Scene 5: Stellar Escrow Payment & Anchor Off-ramp (Duration: ~15s)
> **Text for TTS:**
> "Once the user confirms the payment, Freighter signs the transaction on-chain. After confirming the transfer to the escrow pool, Lintas initiates a real-world anchor off-ramp transaction, producing a verifiable on-chain redemption transaction hash."

---

### Scene 6: Payment Checkout & Polling Settlement (Duration: ~15s)
> **Text for TTS:**
> "The bridge engine automatically creates a local fiat payment checkout. The app polls the payment status in real-time. Once paid, the progress steps update instantly, and the payout status turns green, confirming successful settlement directly to the merchant's bank account."

---

### Scene 7: Transaction History & Network Isolation (Duration: ~15s)
> **Text for TTS:**
> "Finally, all transaction history logs are safely stored. The history list dynamically filters and isolates transactions based on the active network, showing only Testnet entries when on Testnet, and Mainnet entries when switched to Mainnet. Lintas makes crypto spending simple, fast, and regulation-friendly."
